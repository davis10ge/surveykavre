About Us

Survey Office Kavrepalanchowk is located at Dhulikhel which is about 30 km southeast from the capital Kathmandu. It is only one office that oversees the whole process of parcel fragmentation process in Kavrepalanchowk District which covers an area of 1396 sq km. It provides other services too like the computer prints of parcel maps, copy of field books , copy of plot register,etc.
